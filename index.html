MISSION PROTOCOLS

RULE 01 // KILL RESTRICTION

Jab koi Impostor kisi specific player ka shapeshift leta hai, toh usko sirf usi player ko kill karna allowed hai.


Example Scenario:
Agar Impostor RODY ka shapeshift leta hai, toh sirf RODY ko hi kill kar sakta hai.


Shapeshift use karte waqt kisi aur player ko kill karna allowed nahi hai. Jab tak kill confirm nahi hota, body report allowed nahi hai.

RULE 02 // PENALTIES & CONDUCT

  • Wrong Kill: Agar Impostor galat player ko kill karta hai, toh usse immediately match se KICK / ELIMINATE kar diya jayega.
  • Respect: Staff aur Organizers ki respect karna zaroori hai. Agar misbehavior kiya toh PERMANENT BAN lag sakta hai.

RULE 03 // GAME CONFIG

  • Har Team mein sirf 2 Members honge.
  • Game mein 4 Engineers honge (No other roles). Vent cooldown: 15s.
  • Har player ko Impostor banne ka sirf 1 chance milega.
  • Impostor ko Vote out karna allowed nahi hai.

CLASSIFIED // POINT SYSTEM

  • IMPOSTOR: +2 Points har successful kill ke liye.
  • IMPOSTOR BONUS: +4 Points agar saare Crewmates ko maar diya (Full Wipe).
  • CREWMATE: +3 Points agar poora round survive kiya aur tasks finish kiye.
NOTE: Agar Impostor ne first kill he wrong kill kiya (Rule 1 break kiya), toh Crewmates ko points nahi milenge.
