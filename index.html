RULE 01 // KILL RESTRICTION

ENGLISH: When an Impostor shapeshifts into a specific player, they are only allowed to kill that specific player. No other kills until the target is eliminated.
HINGLISH: Jab Impostor kisi player ka shape leta hai, toh sirf usi player ko kill kar sakta hai. Tab tak kisi aur ko kill karna allowed nahi.

Example / Udahran: Impostor RODY ka shapeshift leta hai → sirf RODY ko hi kill karega.

⚡ Important: Jab tak kill confirm nahi ho jata, body report allowed nahi.

RULE 02 // PENALTIES & CONDUCT

ENGLISH: Wrong kill → immediate elimination from match. Disrespect → permanent ban.
HINGLISH: Galat kill kiya toh match se turant kick. Staff se badtameezi → permanent ban.
  • Wrong kill = direct elimination (koi chance nahi)
  • Misbehavior / gaali = server se hamesha ke liye nikala jayega

RULE 03 // GAME CONFIG

ENGLISH: 2 members per team. 4 Engineers (vent 15s). Each player gets Impostor only once. Impostor cannot be voted out. Discord VC mandatory.
HINGLISH: Har team mein sirf 2 members. 4 Engineers honge (vent cooldown 15s). Har player sirf ek baar Impostor banega. Impostor ko vote out karna allowed nahi. Discord VC join karna zaroori hai.

CLASSIFIED // POINT SYSTEM

ENGLISH POINTS:
• Impostor kill: +2
• Full crew wipe: +4 bonus
• Crewmate survive + tasks: +3
HINGLISH SCORING:
• Har kill ke liye Impostor ko 2 point.
• Saare crewmates khatam kar diye (full wipe) → 4 extra point.
• Crewmate safe bacha aur tasks kiye → 3 point.
⚠️ NOTE: Agar Impostor ne Rule 1 tod di (wrong kill), toh us round me crewmates ko koi point nahi milega.
