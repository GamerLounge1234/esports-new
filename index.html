01 // KILL RESTRICTION

When an Impostor shapeshifts into a player, they are restricted to killing ONLY that specific player.


Example: If you shapeshift into RODY, you can only eliminate RODY.


Killing anyone else while shapeshifted is prohibited. Body reporting is NOT allowed until the correct kill is confirmed.

Jab Impostor kisi player ka shapeshift leta hai, toh usko SIRF usi player ko kill karna allowed hai.


Example: Agar tum RODY ka shapeshift lete ho, toh sirf RODY ko hi kill kar sakte ho.


Shapeshift ke time kisi aur ko kill karna mana hai. Jab tak kill confirm nahi hota, body report karna completely MANA hai.

02 // CONDUCT & PENALTIES

  • Wrong Kill: Killing the wrong target results in an immediate KICK / ELIMINATION from the match.
  • Respect: Respect the Staff and Organizers. Any toxic behavior will result in a PERMANENT BAN.
  • Wrong Kill: Galat player ko kill karne par turant match se KICK / ELIMINATE kar diya jayega.
  • Respect: Staff aur Organizers ki respect karein. Toxic behavior par sidha PERMANENT BAN lagega.

03 // GAME CONFIG

  • Only 2 Members per squad.
  • 4 Engineers total. Vent cooldown: 15s.
  • Each player gets exactly 1 chance to be the Impostor.
  • Voting out the Impostor is strictly BANNED.
  • Joining the Discord VC is mandatory.
  • Har team mein sirf 2 Members honge.
  • Lobby mein 4 Engineers rahenge. Vent cooldown: 15s.
  • Har player ko Impostor banne ka sirf 1 chance milega.
  • Impostor ko vote out karna strictly BANNED hai.
  • Sabhi ko Discord VC join karna compulsory hai.

04 // POINT SYSTEM

  • IMPOSTOR: +2 Points per successful kill.
  • FULL WIPE BONUS: +4 Points for killing all Crewmates.
  • CREWMATE: +3 Points for surviving the round and finishing tasks.
NOTE: If the Impostor breaks Rule 1 on their first kill, Crewmates get 0 points.
  • IMPOSTOR: Har sahi kill par +2 Points.
