MISSION PROTOCOLS

RULE 01 // KILL RESTRICTION

ENGLISH:
When an Impostor takes the shape of a specific player, they are only allowed to kill that specific player.
Example: If Impostor shapeshifts as RODY, they can only kill RODY. No other kill allowed until the correct kill is confirmed. Body report not allowed until kill confirmed.
HINGLISH:
Jab Impostor kisi specific player ka shapeshift leta hai, toh usko sirf usi player ko kill karna allowed hai.
Jaise: agar Impostor RODY ka shapeshift kare, toh sirf RODY ko maarega. Shapeshift use karte waqt kisi aur ko kill karna allowed nahi. Jab tak kill confirm nahi hota, body report allowed nahi.

RULE 02 // PENALTIES & CONDUCT

ENGLISH:
  • Wrong Kill: If Impostor kills wrong player → immediate KICK / ELIMINATE from match.
  • Respect: Staff & organizers must be respected. Misbehavior → PERMANENT BAN.
HINGLISH:
  • Galat Kill: Agar Impostor ne wrong player kill kiya → turant match se KICK / ELIMINATE.
  • Respect: Staff aur organizers ki respect karna zaroori. Misbehavior → PERMANENT BAN.

RULE 03 // GAME CONFIG

ENGLISH:
  • Each team: 2 Members only.
  • 4 Engineers (No other roles). Vent cooldown: 15s.
  • Each player gets only 1 chance to be Impostor.
  • Impostor cannot be voted out.
  • Discord VC mandatory.
HINGLISH:
  • Har team mein sirf 2 Members honge.
  • Game mein 4 Engineers (No other roles). Vent cooldown: 15s.
  • Har player ko Impostor banne ka sirf 1 chance milega.
  • Impostor ko vote out karna allowed nahi hai.
  • Discord VC join karna mandatory hai

CLASSIFIED // POINT SYSTEM

ENGLISH:
  • IMPOSTOR: +2 Points per kill.
  • BONUS: +4 Points for full crew wipe.
  • CREWMATE: +3 Points survive + tasks done.
Note: If Impostor breaks rule 1 (wrong kill), crewmates get 0 points.
HINGLISH:
  • IMPOSTOR: +2 Points har kill ke liye.
  • BONUS: +4 Points agar saare Crewmates ko maar diya (Full Wipe).
  • CREWMATE: +3 Points agar poora round survive + tasks finish.
ध्यान: Agar Impostor ne first kill wrong kiya (Rule 1), toh Crewmates ko points nahi milenge.
